# US-01: Page Load and Identity

## User Story

**As a** visitor  
**I want** the homepage to load and display the correct title and Troop 23 branding  
**So that** I know I am on the right site.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] The homepage loads successfully (HTTP 200 or equivalent).
- [ ] The page title (browser tab / `<title>`) reflects Troop 23 and BSA (e.g., "Troop 23, Bay Ridge, Brooklyn, New York, Boy Scouts of America, BSA" or equivalent).
- [ ] The main heading (H1) is visible and contains "Troop 23" and Brooklyn/BSA context.
- [ ] No critical errors prevent the page from rendering above the fold.

## Notes

- Scope: https://www.t23b.org/
- Relevant for: smoke testing, regression.
